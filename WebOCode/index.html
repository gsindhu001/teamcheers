Home Furnishings and Amenities

Welcome to Apt Furnitures!


Our Company

We are a rapidly growing sustainable furniture brand company that focuses on making your homes bright and beautiful. We are keen on designing your homes just the way you want them to be. Our employees who expertize in interior designing patiently analyze and design your homes by making sure that they pick the right kind of furniture to match your walls and room. They help you build cozy, conscious, rich, and elegant homes that match your ideas and keep them affordable for you!



Our Sustainable Furniture

Our furniture is custom and hand-made, and one-of-a-kind pieces, and the wood is free from harmful chemicals like lead and formaldehyde and retardants. The appliances and furnishings are delivered in minimal and proper packaging with recyclable cardboard pieces. We always try to explain to our customers the benefits of natural air purifiers in the homes and always try to add low-maintenance home plants into the interior designing requirements of our customers.



Our Story

We indeed spend most of our lives in our beautiful homes and find peace in coming back home. We loved designing our home. We also used to help our friends pick the right colors for their home walls and helped them choose their matching furniture, home decor, and good home appliances, and them loving our ideas gave us happiness. These small moments brought us the idea of building our own company that would have all the necessary items for the home in one place and still making sure it's all safe and sustainable. That was when Me, Ross, and my wife, Rachel, came together and decided to start our very own "Apt Furniture" company in 2009, with our savings. Now we are delivering and helping people worldwide create bright and beautiful homes according to our customer's ideas and matching the imaginations of their lovely place.
